# Business Insights

## Market Overview

- Zealand generated the highest overall housing sales.
- Bornholm contributed the lowest overall sales volume.
- Offer price and purchase price show a strong positive relationship.
- Sales performance varies significantly across different sales types.

## Regional Insights

- Regional housing markets behave differently in terms of pricing and growth.
- Jutland and Zealand dominate the housing market.
- Average price per square meter differs across regions.

## House Type Insights

- Farm properties have the largest average property size.
- Apartment properties have the highest price per square meter.
- Summerhouses have relatively smaller sizes and lower prices.

## Time-Based Insights

- Year-over-year sales growth fluctuates depending on sales type.
- Latest year and quarter indicators help monitor recent market activity.
- YTD sales provide cumulative performance tracking.

## Key Influencers

Factors that influence purchase price:

- Property age
- House type
- Region
- Property size (SQM)
- Economic indicators
- Sales type

## Conclusion

The Denmark housing market demonstrates strong regional variation, differences across house categories, and changing sales trends over time. Interactive dashboards help identify pricing behavior, market performance, and economic influences that can support data-driven decision making.
