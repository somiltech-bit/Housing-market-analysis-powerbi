## Dashboard Pages

### 1. House Market Overview

Visuals:
- Median Sales Price Change by Region
- Units Sold (Latest Year & Quarter)
- Offer Price vs Purchase Price
- YOY Sales Growth by Sales Type

### 2. Sales Performance

Visuals:
- Sales by Region
- Key Influencers Analysis
- Offer to SQM Ratio by Sales Type
- Average Price SQM by Region
- YTD Sales Table

### 3. House Type Analysis

Visuals:
- Average Offer vs Purchase Price
- Inflation, Interest Rate and Yield Analysis
- SQM vs SQM Price Analysis
- Interactive Filters
